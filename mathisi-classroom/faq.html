Frequently Asked Question

  • What is the Mathisi Mini Classroom?
  • Mathisi Mini Classroom is a virtual learning platform for focused studies on the important subjects High School Students must be excellent at, in other to excel and to move to the next class or write School Certificate Exams at Junior and Senior Levels.
  • Who can access learning on the Mathisi Mini Classroom?
  • The mini classroom is open to all secondary school students and it has been created to be accessed from virtually anywhere in the world via internet-enabled computers and mobile devices.
  • Is there a specific age for students on Mathisi before being allowed to sign up?
  • The ideal age for students getting into secondary school is between 10 to 12 years old.
  • How do I sign up?
  • Visit app home page and click on the sign up link for students. Even though the process is simple enough for most students, we encourage parents to help their children and wards in the signup process.
  • Who can become a teacher on Mathisi?
  • Teachers are selected through a strict and professional recruitment process that focuses on their accomplishments and pedigree as teachers. To apply to be a teacher, contact the app administrators.
  • Can I start learning any time?
  • Classes are tailored to follow, as much as possible, the government-approved school calendar and this to ensure that students can retain what they learn and use it to augment what is being learnt at school and the semester system like in a regular school.
  • Is online learning really beneficial?
  • In most cases, online course delivery works well with the exception of students for whom online learning may be totally unsuitable. However other factors come into play such as student motivation, familiarity with the computer and smart devices, and the ability to work independently, meet deadlines and virtual interaction and work with others.
  • What courses are available on Mathisi?
  • Mathematics
  • English Language
  • Literature
  • Further Mathematics
  • Biology
  • Economics
  • How are classes delivered?
  • Classes are delivered through a blend of live lecture sessions and webinars as well as classwork, quizzes, games and interactive engagement.
  • Previously signed in but forgot password?
  • Select the forgot password link on the sign up page to authenticate your email address and you will be provided steps on how to change your password.
